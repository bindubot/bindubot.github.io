About us

Bindubot Communication is a social branding agency that operates from Kolkata and Delhi. The agency provides business solutions to corporate houses by making brands social.
Social branding is a unique concept that very effectively and creatively contributes in creating social value for corporate houses, NGOs and the Government, both in terms of generating social good will value for the brand and as well as direct product sales.
Bindubot Communication has expert services for creative social advertisements, social campaigns and producing creative musical concerts/ productions that add the much-needed human touch to corporate brands that create a long-lasting impression in the customers’ mind-space.
Bindubot Communication also brings changes in mass perceptions and builds public opinion for various brands and uses a variety of social groups and communities for unique brand value creation. All Bindubot campaigns are designed to get social issues and problems branded in people’s mind.

The agency has a very well-formulated model and creative marketing strategy that expands the market scope for direct sales, especially in exploring and introducing the socially created brands to a new segment of customers that the corporate sales representatives are hardly able to reach. Thus it gives a sustainable business growth for the brand simultaneously.
Hence, social branding done by Bindubot Communication has very long-term benefits for corporate houses.
